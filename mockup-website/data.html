📈 Age-Related Trends

Insurance interest exhibits clear career-stage patterns, peaking among customers in their prime earning years (31-50) when financial planning and risk mitigation become higher priorities.

  • Young Adults (20-30): Lowest interest, potential for education campaigns
  • Early/Mid Career (31-50): Peak interest, primary target market
  • Seniors (60+): Moderate interest, value-focused messaging

🎯 Targeting Strategy

Focus primary marketing efforts on 31-50 age group while developing specialized awareness campaigns for underserved younger demographics.
