Key Problem Areas

💸

Financial Vulnerability

Without insurance, a single accident can push families into poverty. Vehicles are often people's most valuable assets, making their loss financially catastrophic. Emergency repair costs can exceed monthly income.

🚫

Economic Disruption

Many individuals depend on vehicles for employment or operating small businesses. Lack of insurance protection threatens employment stability and economic participation in the community.

🌍

Community-Wide Impact

Under-insurance creates a ripple effect throughout communities, reducing overall economic resilience and social stability. It affects not just individuals, but entire neighborhoods and local economies.
