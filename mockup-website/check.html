How Our AI Model Works

Our machine learning model uses XGBoost algorithm trained on 381,109 customer records to predict insurance interest. Key factors include:

  • ✅ Vehicle Damage History: Most significant predictor (11x higher interest if damaged)
  • ✅ Age Demographics: Peak interest in 31-50 age group (>30% interest rate)
  • ✅ Regional Patterns: Location-based risk assessment
  • ✅ Financial Profile: Premium budget and payment history
  • ✅ Vehicle Characteristics: Age and condition of vehicle
