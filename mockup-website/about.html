About InsureCheck

Transforming insurance accessibility through AI-powered data analytics and community-focused solutions for a financially secure future.

Our Mission

InsureCheck is a data-driven platform designed to address a critical community challenge: the lack of adequate vehicle insurance. By applying data analytics and machine learning, our project aims to identify individuals who are most likely to need and be interested in vehicle insurance.

The platform helps insurance providers and community organizations conduct targeted awareness campaigns and outreach, ultimately fostering greater financial security within the community. This project transforms raw data into an actionable tool that serves both business needs and broader social stability goals.

We believe in leveraging technology for social good, creating solutions that not only drive business success but also contribute to community resilience and financial inclusion.

Sustainable Development Goals Alignment

🎯 SDG 1: No Poverty

Insurance serves as an essential safety net, preventing families from falling into poverty due to unexpected vehicle accidents and repair costs. Without insurance, a single accident can devastate a family's financial stability.

💼 SDG 8: Decent Work

Many people depend on their vehicles for work or operating small businesses. Insurance protection guarantees employment stability and ongoing economic participation in the community.

🏗️ SDG 9: Innovation & Infrastructure

We leverage technology to increase access to financial services like insurance, contributing to the development of a more robust economic infrastructure and promoting financial inclusion.

  • SMOTE for class balancing
